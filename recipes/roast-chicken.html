Roast Chicken

Description

\

This is so easy it's stupid.

Ingredients

  • One 4 lb whole chicken, rinsed
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F
  2. Place chicken into a roasting pan and insert lemon halves into the cavity; spread kosher salt over outside of chicken.
  3. Roast in the preheated oven until skin is browned and crisp, the juices run clear, and an instant-read meat thermometer inserted into the thickest part of a thigh reads at least 160 degrees F, about 1 hour. Let chicken rest for 15 minutes before carving.
